## PATENT 1: Metabolic Circuit-Breaking for Decentralized Logic Matrices
**Focus:** Stability and Self-Protection in Parallel Computing.

### 1. Abstract
A method and apparatus for managing computational stability in decentralized matrices through a local metabolic budget. Each logic cell (protein) possesses an independent energy state that governs its ability to process or propagate signals. High-frequency activation triggers an automatic "Paralytic State" to prevent chaotic feedback loops and physical hardware damage.

### 2. Primary Claims
- **Energy-Gated Firing:** A logic gate that only permits signal propagation if its internal energy register exceeds a specific threshold.
- **Cost-Weighted Logic:** Every computational event (excitation) deducts a specific value from the local metabolic budget.
- **Autonomous Recovery:** A decentralized state machine that transitions cells into a non-responsive "rest mode" for energy replenishment.

### 3. Novelty vs. Prior Art
- **Vs. Neural Networks:** Standard SNNs don't have per-node metabolic collapse; they suffer from "Vanishing/Exploding Gradients." This system uses physical energy as a regulator.
- **Vs. Cellular Automata:** Traditional CA (like Game of Life) is infinite-resource logic. This system is resource-constrained.

## PATENT 2: Multi-Modal Bio-Resonant Signal Extraction (The Sentinel Logic)
**Focus:** Filtering life-signs from high-noise environments without a CPU.

### 1. Abstract
A system for extracting periodic signals (e.g., heartbeats, rhythmic motion) from noisy multi-modal data using decentralized "Cooperative Resonance." Logic cells act as frequency-specific filters that share confidence values with neighbors to achieve a stable signal lock-on.

### 2. Primary Claims
- **Cooperative Resonance Spreading:** A mechanism where cells that detect a frequency match actively boost the resonance registers of neighboring cells.
- **Lateral Noise Suppression:** Once a cell locks onto a resonant rhythm, it sends an inhibitory signal to suppress the "raw" excitation of neighbors, effectively erasing noise from the perception field.
- **Multi-Modal Target Fusion:** The simultaneous processing of visual, acoustic, and RF signals within a single logic cell to generate a unified target confidence.

### 3. Novelty vs. Prior Art
- **Vs. FFT/DSP:** Eliminates the need for centralized frequency analysis. The "Filtering" is an emergent property of the matrix.
